Learn More About Our Work And Our Activities

Annai Educational and Charity Trust is committed to supporting underprivileged communities through various educational and charitable initiatives. We provide free educational materials, financial aid for students, and healthcare programs. Our efforts include rehabilitating child laborers, offering medical services to the elderly, and establishing care centers for orphans and the destitute. We also focus on environmental sustainability through tree planting, promote vocational training, and support cultural and social justice causes. Our trust operates with a non-profit ethos, ensuring that all benefits are accessible to individuals regardless of caste, religion, or background.

vision and mission of our trust

Annai Educational and Charity Trust is dedicated to fostering educational and charitable initiatives that address the needs of the underprivileged and marginalized sections of society. Our trustâ€™s core mission revolves around enhancing educational opportunities by providing free school supplies, uniforms, and financial assistance for school and college fees to deserving students. We are committed to improving community health through organized healthcare programs and addressing critical issues such as child and bonded labor by facilitating rehabilitation and welfare initiatives. Our efforts extend to the establishment of rehabilitation centers, educational institutions for the disabled, and providing medical services to the elderly, including blood and eye donation drives. We also prioritize environmental sustainability by conducting tree plantation drives and ensuring their maintenance.

Our trust plays a vital role in supporting festive celebrations by providing free meals and operating homes, asylums, and daycare centers for various vulnerable groups, including orphans, street children, and the elderly. Additionally, we support the development of vocational skills and professional training, particularly for girls, and actively engage in promoting and implementing government schemes related to health, nutrition, and environmental conservation. We are committed to cultural preservation, social justice, and improving the quality of life for disadvantaged communities through legal aid, housing programs, and various community development activities. By raising funds and collaborating with government bodies, foreign agencies, and local donors, our trust ensures that all activities are carried out without profit motives, maintaining our focus on the welfare and upliftment of those in need.
