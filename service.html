Students Education Fees

We offer scholarships to underprivileged students to cover their school and college fees. This support helps alleviate the financial burden on families and enables students to focus on their education, paving the way for a brighter future.

Planting trees

Our tree-planting initiatives aim to enhance local environments by increasing green spaces and improving air quality. By planting trees in communities, we contribute to environmental sustainability and the health of our planet.

Disability Aids

We provide essential aids and equipment to individuals with disabilities, such as mobility aids and vision support devices. This assistance helps improve their quality of life and promotes greater independence and accessibility.

Free medical camps

We organize free medical camps to offer health check-ups, consultations, and treatments to underserved communities. These camps ensure that individuals receive necessary medical care and health education at no cost.

Care of Abandoned Elderly

Our program provides care and support to abandoned elderly individuals. We offer them shelter, meals, medical care, and companionship, ensuring they live with dignity and receive the attention they need.

vocational training to woman

We offer vocational training programs for women in skills like tailoring, soap production, and incense making. These programs aim to empower women with the skills needed for financial independence and self-sufficiency.
