About Us

How Our Trust Work

Annai Educational and Charity Trust is dedicated to advancing educational and charitable causes through a broad range of initiatives aimed at uplifting underprivileged communities. Our mission encompasses providing free educational materials and financial aid to needy students, organizing health care and educational programs, and supporting the rehabilitation of child laborers and disabled individuals. We are committed to promoting community welfare through tree plantations, medical services, and free food distribution during festivals. Our trust also focuses on the establishment of homes and care centers for the aged, orphans, and destitute individuals, while striving to enhance the quality of life for the poor and marginalized through professional training and skill development programs. Additionally, we actively engage in cultural preservation, social justice advocacy, and environmental sustainability. Our activities are inclusive and non-profit-oriented, ensuring that the benefits of our work reach individuals regardless of caste, religion, or background.

Services

Students Education Fees

We provide financial support for school and college fees to underprivileged students, helping them overcome financial barriers and pursue their education with fewer worries.

Planting trees

We organize and support tree planting activities to promote environmental sustainability and enhance local ecosystems. By planting trees, we aim to combat climate change, improve air quality, and create greener, healthier communities.

Disability Aids

We offer essential aids and equipment to individuals with disabilities, enhancing their mobility and independence. Our support includes providing wheelchairs, hearing aids, and other assistive devices to improve quality of life and ensure greater accessibility and inclusion.

Free medical camps

We organize free medical camps to provide essential health services to underserved communities. These camps offer medical check-ups, diagnostic tests, and treatment, ensuring access to healthcare for those who might otherwise be unable to afford it.

Care of Abandoned Elderly

We provide compassionate care and support for abandoned elderly individuals, offering them a safe, nurturing environment. Our services include basic needs such as food, shelter, and medical care, ensuring dignity and quality of life for those in need.

given vocational training to woman

We provide specialized vocational training for women in areas such as tailoring, soap production, and incense making. These programs are designed to equip women with valuable skills.
